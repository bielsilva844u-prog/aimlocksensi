# **App Name**: Clear Canvas

## Core Features:

- Blank Page Display: Render a clean, minimalist blank page with essential structural elements (header, main content area, footer).
- Responsive Layout: Ensure the blank page adapts seamlessly across various device sizes and orientations.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual design emphasizes clarity and minimalism, drawing inspiration from the concept of a pristine blank slate. The primary color is a calming medium blue, HSL(200, 40%, 50%), converting to RGB hex '#4D91B3'. This hue evokes a sense of tranquility and professionalism, used for interactive elements or important text to provide clear contrast against the light background.
- The background color is a very light, almost white, subtle blue, HSL(200, 15%, 98%), converting to RGB hex '#F7FBFC'. It's visually of the same hue as the primary but heavily desaturated to promote a clean, airy feel consistent with a light color scheme.
- The accent color is a rich, darker teal, HSL(170, 50%, 30%), converting to RGB hex '#267366'. It's analogous to the primary blue but with a shift in hue, and offers a strong, contrasting highlight for key actions or significant dividers.
- The chosen font for both headlines and body text is 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif. Its modern, objective, and neutral aesthetic perfectly complements the minimalist 'blank page' concept, ensuring readability and a clean visual hierarchy.
- Functional and minimalist line icons should be used where necessary, maintaining the app's clean and uncluttered appearance. Icons should be easily discernible without distracting from the content (or lack thereof).
- The layout is extremely simple and focused, utilizing generous whitespace around a centered content area. A subtle header and footer define the page boundaries, ensuring an unobtrusive frame for the 'blank canvas'.
- Animations should be minimal and understated, focusing on smooth transitions for elements appearing or disappearing, if any. The aim is not to distract but to subtly enhance user experience through fluid motion.
